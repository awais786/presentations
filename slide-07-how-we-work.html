How We Work

Arbisoft Open Source Team · Our playbook for building a credible contribution program
Open source success isn't accidental. We set up a structured playbook from day one - pick the right repos, build community presence, and ship consistently.
01
Pick Focus Repos
Started with 4 strategic repos aligned to Arbisoft's delivery stack - not random drive-by PRs.
Wagtail Django Oscar OpenEdX Strapi
02
Assign Ownership
Each engineer owns a program. One repo, one lead - accountability, continuity, and deep context over time.
03
Embed in Community
Joined forums, Slack channels, mailing lists. Attended community calls. Learned the people, the culture, and the roadmap.
04
Discover & Plan
Triaged issues, studied long-term feature plans, and mapped contribution opportunities to team skills and interests.
05
Ship Pull Requests
Start small, build trust. Pick issues, submit PRs, iterate on reviews. Merged PRs compound into credibility.
06
Internal Sync
Weekly team sessions - blockers, learnings, review patterns, and cross-repo knowledge sharing. The team lifts the team.
Targeted repos · owned programs · community presence · consistent delivery
