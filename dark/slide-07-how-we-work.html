Assign Ownership
Each engineer owns a program. One repo, one lead — accountability, continuity, deep context.
03
Embed in Community
Joined forums, Slack, community calls. Learned the culture and roadmap.
04
Discover & Plan
Triaged issues, studied long-term feature plans, mapped opportunities to team skills.
05
Ship Pull Requests
Start small, build trust. Pick issues, submit PRs, iterate. Merged PRs compound into credibility.
06
Internal Sync
Weekly team sessions — blockers, learnings, review patterns, knowledge sharing.
